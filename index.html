Consciousness Engineering Labs

CE Labs aims to understand consciousness and how to engineer it. We design experiments to probe the mechanics and structure of experience and develop neurotechnologies to interface with it.

In the coming months, we will run a set of tFUS experiments.

Over time, we will follow whatever approach seems most promising. With a small number of experiments, we will shed light on the central problems of consciousness and begin building neurotechnologies that give humans broad control over conscious experience.
